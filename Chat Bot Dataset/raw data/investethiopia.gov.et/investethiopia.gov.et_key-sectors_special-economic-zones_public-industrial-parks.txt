INVEST IN ETHIOPIA / PUBLIC INDUSTRIAL PARKSPublic Industrial ParksDriving industrialization through integration―a manufacturing model at its best 
Contact UsPaving the way for investorsOur industrial park development program is driven by our desire to create export-oriented, sector-specialized, vertically-integrated and sustainable industries. Till now, we have built 16 public industrial parks and over 300 companies have made them their home.

We invite investor looking to develop industrial parks jointly with our government to engage in public-private partnership. Our industrial growth has increase demand for a manufacturing space that is ready-for-business and offers quick entry to market. We are keen to support investors exploring this investment opportunity.
